Rockt was a geopresence game that took place on Toronto's streetcar network. Every time you rode streetcar, you could check-in, and check out every time you left. As you rode, you would be able to purchase your cars, and levy fees on others. The goal was to amass a fleet and control Toronto!

For a variety of reasons, including the fact that stillinbeta no longer resides in Toronto, Rockt has been shut down. Never trust a webapp you don't pay for ;)
